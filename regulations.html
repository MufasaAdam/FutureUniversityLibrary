Libray Regulations

Purpose and Authority :

  • The regulations of e-library of the Future University are a supplement to the current edition of the university statutes .
  • They are intended to :-
    1. Provide for the internal governance of the library.
    2. Define further the rights and responsibilities of the library and the users.
    3. Help the library achieving its goals.
  • However , in case of conflict of the regulations with statutes , the latter will PREVAIL.
  • The regulations shall be effective from the date of its endorsement by the authorities concerned in the Future University .
